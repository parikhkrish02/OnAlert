ABOUT US

ONALERT personalised notifier

Competitive programming is very important for Tech students as it increases chances of getting them hired. A lot of Students face issues of missing contests. With the need of such platform which helps students to get notified at regular intervals for contests across various platforms like Codechef, Codeforces, etc. We came up with idea of “OnAlert” which helps students to compete on the various platforms by regularly updating them about contests. ONALERT is customizable web notifier which allows user to select particular platforms and then update them about information regarding it at regular intervals.
